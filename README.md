# Club Tucán - Official Website 📚⚽🌱

Welcome to the official repository of **Club Tucán**, a youth center in Guatemala dedicated to the integral formation of young men (ages 14-22). 

## Our Mission
We aim to empower the next generation through:
* **Academic Excellence:** Developing professional study habits.
* **Sports & Leisure:** Promoting health and teamwork through football and healthy hobbies. ⚽
* **Character Building:** Fostering lasting friendships and human virtues.
* **Spiritual Growth:** Optional Christian formation entrusted to the **Opus Dei Prelature**.

## Goal
This website serves as a digital hub for students and parents to access our educational and sports programs in a safe, dynamic environment.
